Sam recipe for The Best Cinnamon Roolls

Ingredient

For the dough

  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown suger
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,suger,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix util combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with wrap. Leave it somewhare to rise for 2 hours.
